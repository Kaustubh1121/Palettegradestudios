About

PaletteGrade Studios is Pune's dedicated post-production house specializing in Color Grading, Digital Intermediate (D.I.) and Cinema Mastering.

Powered by DaVinci Resolve Studio, we take your project from RAW footage processing through to final DCP, OTT and broadcast mastering, delivering studio-grade cinematic quality at every stage.

We deliver studio-grade quality to every project locally in Pune and remotely across India.
